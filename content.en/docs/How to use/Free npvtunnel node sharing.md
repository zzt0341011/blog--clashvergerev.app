## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are these free nodes permanently available?

No. Free proxy nodes may expire, become overloaded, or stop working at any time.

### Does NPV Tunnel support VLESS and Trojan links?

Yes. NPV Tunnel supports VLESS, VMess, Trojan, Shadowsocks, SOCKS5, and other mainstream protocols.

### How do I import a node into NPV Tunnel?

Open **Configs → + → Import Config from Clipboard**, then paste the configuration link and save it.

### Are free nodes suitable for long-term use?

Free nodes are usually unstable and may have speed limitations. For better performance and reliability, consider using a paid proxy provider.
